About Entrepreneurship & Venture Funding

Entrepreneurship is the process of designing, launching, and running a new business, typically a small business. It involves taking on financial risks in the hope of profit. Venture funding provides capital to early-stage startups that are deemed to have high growth potential.

Resources for Entrepreneurs

  • Books: "The Lean Startup" by Eric Ries, "Zero to One" by Peter Thiel
  • Websites: Y Combinator, TechCrunch
  • Videos: TED Talks on entrepreneurship
